About Me

My name is Erik. I'm originally from Denver, Colorado. My family moved to New Mexico when I was seven. I lived on the Navajo Indian Reservation until I was fourteen. We then moved to Texas, where I went to High School. After High School, I joined the Army and spent time in Maryland and Germany. Once I was done with the Army I moved back to Texas where I continued going to College. In 1994 I moved to Seattle.
